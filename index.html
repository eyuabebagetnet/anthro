INTRODUCTION

Anthropology is the study of mankind(anthropos).Etymologically, anthropology comes from the word anthropos meaning man and logos meaning knowledge. Anthropology looks at humans as something complex in terms of physical,emotional,social,and cultural complexity. Anthropology also refer to the science of humans and their culture. since anthropology is one of the social sciences, of course it is a necessity,and undoubtedly very important considering humans as social creatures can not possibly loos intracting with each other. studying anthropology will create tolerance and apeaceful life.
